About The Developer

Hi. I’m Chris Miller, the creator of RetirementOdds.com.

I recently retired at age 54 from my career as a software engineer. To decide when I could retire, I used the same methodology that you’ll find here in this tool. I built it in spreadsheet form and honed it with more and more detail over the course of 10+ years. I did this because there was no other free tool on the web that let me input my data the way I thought about it, and handled all the special cases in my situation that could impact my results.

Now, as a retiree with more free time on my hands, I’m using my skills to bring this calculator to the web for others to use. I truly hope you find it useful in your path to or through retirement!

Oh, by the way, I also caught some attention from a social media post I did, and that led to a feature about me in Fortune Magazine. 😀.
